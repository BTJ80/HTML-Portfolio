The Best Movies According to Brandi

My top 3 movies of all time

Breakin

A movie about break dancing in the 80's. The main character teaches a professional suburban woman street dancing,in order to win a dance competition.

Scarface

This movie is about a drug dealer in the late 70's.His arrogance leads to his dismise.

The Color Purple

A story about the life of woman from the deep South in the early 1900's. She is navigates through racism,domestic violence,and losing the children she birthed as an adolescent.
